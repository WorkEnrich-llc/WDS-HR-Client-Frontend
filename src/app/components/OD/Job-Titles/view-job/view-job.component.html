Job Description

The Chief Operating Officer (COO) is responsible for overseeing the day-to-day administrative and operational functions of the company. Reporting directly to the Chief Executive Officer (CEO), the COO collaborates closely with other executive leaders to implement strategies, improve organizational efficiency, and ensure cross-departmental alignment. The COO plays a critical role in driving business growth, maintaining operational excellence, and executing the company’s long-term vision.

Job Requirements

  • Proven experience as COO or in a similar executive role.
  • Strong understanding of business functions such as HR, Finance, IT, Marketing, and Operations.
  • Demonstrated leadership and people management skills.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities.
  • Strong strategic and analytical thinking
  • Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Management, or a related field (MBA preferred).
  • Experience in managing budgets, resources, and operational strategy.
  • Familiarity with performance metrics and reporting tools.
